# Using the AI Assistant

## What is the AI Assistant?

The Nexora AI Assistant is your helpful guide that can:
- Answer questions about Nexora features
- Help you learn how to do things
- Provide tips and tricks
- Troubleshoot problems

## How to Use the AI

**Ask a Question**

1. Click on the chat icon or "Ask AI"
2. Type your question in plain English
3. Hit Enter or Send
4. Get an instant answer!

**Examples of Questions You Can Ask:**

- "How do I send a message?"
- "How do I create a group chat?"
- "Can I upload videos?"
- "How do I find people to connect with?"
- "How do I change my password?"
- "What can I post about?"
- "How do I get notifications?"
- "How do I share files?"

## Getting Better Answers

**Be Specific**
- ❌ "How do I use Nexora?"
- ✅ "How do I create a group chat?"

**Ask One Thing at a Time**
- ❌ "How do I message someone and upload files?"
- ✅ "How do I message someone?"

**Use Simple Words**
- ✅ "I can't upload my photo"
- ❌ "There's an issue with the attachment mechanism"

## Types of Questions the AI Can Answer

**How-To Questions**
- "How do I post an update?"
- "How do I add someone to a group?"
- "How do I change my profile picture?"

**Feature Questions**
- "What can I upload?"
- "Who can see my posts?"
- "Do you have video calls?"

**Troubleshooting**
- "My messages aren't sending"
- "I can't download a file"
- "I forgot my password"

**Account Questions**
- "How do I change my password?"
- "How do I delete my account?"
- "What's my username?"

## Chat Tips

**The AI is here to help, so:**
- Ask anything about Nexora
- Don't be shy - no silly questions!
- Rephrase if you don't get a good answer
- Follow up with more questions

**What to expect:**
- Quick answers (usually instant)
- Clear, easy-to-understand language
- Practical, usable advice
- Helpful examples

## Privacy

Your questions to the AI:
- Are private
- Aren't shared with other users
- Stay encrypted

Don't share passwords or sensitive info with the AI!

## When to Ask the AI

**Ask the AI For:**
- Quick how-to questions
- Feature explanations
- Troubleshooting tips
- General Nexora guidance

**Contact Support Instead For:**
- Technical bugs
- Account issues
- Security concerns
- Billing questions

## Sample Conversations

**Q: How do I create a group chat?**
A: Click "New Group", give it a name, select the people you want to add, and send your first message!

**Q: Can I upload videos?**
A: Yes! You can upload videos in messages or posts.

**Q: What do I do if someone is bothering me?**
A: You can block them or report the behavior to our support team. Your safety matters to us!

Enjoy using Nexora!
