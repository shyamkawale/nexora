# Welcome to Nexora!

Nexora is your all-in-one platform to stay connected with your team and community.

## What Can You Do?

**Chat with People**
- Send private messages to colleagues or friends
- Create group chats with multiple people
- See real-time typing indicators
- Share files directly in conversations

**Share & Discuss**
- Post updates, ideas, or announcements
- Comment on other people's posts
- Like posts you find interesting
- Follow trends in your community

**Stay Updated**
- Know when someone is online
- Get instant notifications
- See who's active right now
- Keep track of conversations

**Share Files**
- Upload documents, images, videos
- Share files in messages or posts
- Access files anytime from anywhere
- Easy file download

**Get Help from AI**
- Ask questions about Nexora
- Get quick answers about features
- Learn how to do things
- Get suggestions

## Getting Started

1. **Sign up** with your email
2. **Create your profile** with a photo and bio
3. **Search for colleagues** to connect
4. **Start chatting** or posting

It's that simple! Nexora brings your team together in one place.
