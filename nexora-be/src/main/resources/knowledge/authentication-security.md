# Account & Security

## Creating Your Account

**Sign Up**

1. Go to Nexora and click "Sign Up"
2. Enter your email
3. Create a strong password
4. Verify your email
5. Complete your profile
6. Start chatting!

**Your Profile**
- Add a profile photo
- Write a bio
- Set your status
- Choose privacy settings

## Logging In

**Login Steps**

1. Go to nexora.com
2. Enter your email
3. Enter your password
4. Click "Login"

**Stay Logged In**
- Check "Remember me" to stay logged in
- Uncheck if using a shared computer
- Always logout on public devices

## Password Security

### Creating a Strong Password

Your password should:
- Be at least 8 characters long
- Include uppercase letters (A-Z)
- Include lowercase letters (a-z)
- Include numbers (0-9)
- Include special characters (!@#$)

**Example of a strong password**: MyNexora2024!Team

**Weak passwords to avoid**:
- 12345678 (too simple)
- password (too obvious)
- yourname123 (uses personal info)

### Password Requirements
- **Minimum length**: 8 characters
- **Complexity**: Mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, special chars
- **No dictionary words**: Prevent common passwords
- **No previous passwords**: Don't reuse old passwords

### Password Reset

**Forgot Your Password?**

1. Click "Forgot Password"
2. Enter your email
3. Check your email for a reset link
4. Click the link
5. Create a new password
6. Login with new password

## Keeping Your Account Safe

**Do's:**
✓ Use a unique password for Nexora
✓ Change password every 3-6 months
✓ Logout when done on shared computers
✓ Report suspicious activity immediately
✓ Enable all available security features

**Don'ts:**
✗ Share your password with anyone
✗ Use the same password everywhere
✗ Click suspicious links
✗ Logout on unsecured WiFi (if possible)
✗ Leave your device unattended while logged in

## Privacy Settings

**Who Can See Your Profile?**
- Everyone (public)
- Only connections
- Only you (private)

**Choose what's visible:**
- Your posts
- Your online status
- Your profile picture
- Your bio

## Privacy Tips

- **Be selective**: Don't share personal phone numbers or addresses
- **Think before posting**: Would you say this in person?
- **Manage contacts**: Remove people you no longer want to chat with
- **Report abuse**: Let us know if someone is harassing you
- **Block users**: Block people who make you uncomfortable
