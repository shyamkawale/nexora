# Sharing & Engaging with Posts

## Creating a Post

**Share Your Updates**

1. Click "Create Post" or "What's on your mind?"
2. Type what you want to share
3. Add a photo or file if you want
4. Click "Post"
5. Your post is now live!

**Post Ideas**:
- Quick updates about your day
- Exciting news or announcements
- Interesting articles or resources
- Questions for your team
- Photos or videos
- Achievements or milestones

## Comments

**Adding a Comment**

1. Click "Comment" on a post
2. Type what you think
3. Click "Post"
4. Your comment appears right away

**Tips for Good Comments**
- Ask genuine questions
- Share your experience
- Be respectful
- Keep it positive
- Add value to the conversation

## Likes

Like posts and comments you enjoy!

**How to Like**
- Click the heart/thumbs up icon
- Shows you appreciated the post
- Everyone can see your like

## User Profiles

**Your Profile Shows**
- Your photo
- Your bio
- Posts you've shared
- People following you
- People you follow

**Viewing Other Profiles**
1. Search for someone
2. Click their name
3. See their posts and info
4. Follow them if interested

## Notifications

Stay updated on what's happening!

**You Get Notifications When**:
- Someone likes your post
- Someone comments on your post
- Someone replies to your comment
- Someone mentions you

**Managing Notifications**:
- See all alerts in the notification center
- Mark as read when you've seen them
- Turn on/off notifications in settings

## Following & Unfollowing

**Follow Someone**:
1. Visit their profile
2. Click "Follow"
3. See their posts in your feed

**Unfollow**:
1. Visit their profile or your followers list
2. Click "Unfollow"
3. You won't see their posts anymore

## Your Feed

Your feed shows posts from:
- People you follow
- Trending topics
- People in your organization
- Suggested content

**Tips for a Better Feed**:
- Follow people you're interested in
- Like posts you enjoy
- Comment on things you care about
- Adjust your notification settings

## Privacy & Sharing

**Control Who Sees Your Posts**:
- Public: Everyone on Nexora
- Friends only: Only your followers
- Private: Only me
- Custom: Choose specific people

**Before You Post**:
✓ Think about who should see this  
✓ Check if it's appropriate for your audience  
✓ Avoid sharing sensitive info  
✓ Be respectful and professional

## Engagement Tips

**Build Connections**:
- Comment thoughtfully on others' posts
- Share useful resources
- Ask genuine questions
- Support team members

**Create Great Posts**:
- Be clear and concise
- Use photos when relevant
- Add emojis for personality
- Encourage responses

**Collaborate**:
- Tag people when relevant
- Ask for input
- Share wins and learnings
- Celebrate team achievements
