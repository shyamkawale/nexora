## Understanding Online Status

In Nexora, you can see who's available to chat right now.

**Green Dot = Online**
- Person is actively using Nexora
- Good time to send a message
- They'll see it instantly

**No Dot = Offline**
- Person is not using Nexora right now
- Message will wait for them to return
- They'll see it when they log back in

## How Presence Works

**When You Login**
- Your status changes to online
- Everyone sees the green dot next to your name
- People know they can reach you

**When You Logout or Close the App**
- Your status changes to offline
- The last time you were active is recorded
- People see "Last seen: 2 hours ago"

**Real-Time Updates**
- Status updates instantly
- No delay in seeing who's online
- Perfect for staying connected

## Using Online Status

### In Direct Messages
- See if your friend is online before sending urgent messages
- Know if they're probably available

### In Group Chats
- See who's active in the group
- Know who might reply quickly

### For Scheduling
- Find who's online for a quick discussion
- Schedule meetings when everyone is available

## Why Presence Matters

✓ **Faster communication** - Know if someone is available  
✓ **Better collaboration** - Coordinate with online team members  
✓ **Respect time zones** - See who's active in different regions  
✓ **Real-time updates** - Instant status changes

## Troubleshooting

### Status Not Updating
- Try refreshing the page
- Check your internet connection
- Close and reopen the app

### Always Showing Offline
- Check if you're logged in
- Verify your internet connection
- Contact support if persists
